## ✅ Verified (primary source — Epic Games Store PWS2 article, Destructoid, Will's in-game confirmation)

- Two washer brands: Prime Vista, Urban X
- 8 washers total in 4 tiers (Light, Medium, Heavy, Pro)
- Washer costs:
  - Vista 1500 Free · 2400 $1,000 · 3000 $5,000 · Pro $10,000
  - Urban U1 Free · U2 $1,200 · Max $6,000 · Stream $12,000
- SwirlForce Surf Ace = $500
- Triple-tip nozzle exclusive to Prime Vista Pro
- Double turbo nozzle exclusive to Urban X Stream
- Soap nozzle works with any washer
- 9 total nozzles: 4 standard angle (0°/15°/25°/40°) + 5 specialized (Turbo, Adaptable, Soap, Triple-tip, Double turbo)
- **Cash is the only currency that gates progression**
- **No star unlocks in PWS2**
- **No separate stat upgrades (pressure/flow/tank)** — buying a higher tier is how you "upgrade"
- **PowerWash Points are cosmetic-only**, capped, earned from continued cleaning incl. replays
- **Replays pay PowerWash Points, not cash**
- Access equipment exists: ladders, scaffolds, scissor lifts, abseiling apparatus (level-provided)

## 🟠 Inferred (qualitative synthesis from descriptors, not numerical)

- Vista has wider spray pattern, lower raw pressure
- Urban has higher raw pressure, narrower spread
- 25° is the "default workhorse" / most-used nozzle (multiple guides agree but partly stylistic)
- Triple-tip = "three 0° nozzles combined" (Epic source phrasing)
- Double turbo = "two rotating nozzles in one head"
