# PWS2 Field Guide · Data Audit

Every factual claim currently in the guide, sorted by source confidence.

## ✅ Verified — primary source or strong cross-source agreement

### Washers & shop totals
- Two washer brands: Prime Vista, Urban X
- 8 washers in 4 tiers (Light, Medium, Heavy, Pro), plus SwirlForce Surf Ace
- Washer base costs:
  - Vista 1500 Free · 2400 $1,000 · 3000 $5,000 · Pro $10,000
  - Urban U1 Free · U2 $1,200 · Max $6,000 · Stream $12,000
  - SwirlForce Surf Ace $500
- **Total Maxed Out shop ≈ $70,285** (all 8 washers + SwirlForce + every attachment)
  - Cross-sourced: moyens.net itemized list sums to this; FuzionByte confirmed $35,200 main-washer total
- **Final Career mission: "Mount Rushless"** — pays ~$30,000, enough to cover any remaining gaps

### Nozzles
- 9 total nozzle types: 4 standard angles (0°/15°/25°/40°) + 5 specialized (Turbo, Adaptable, Soap, Triple-tip, Double turbo)
- **Triple-tip exclusive to Vista Pro = $2,500**
- **Double turbo exclusive to Urban X Stream = $3,000**
- **Adaptable nozzle is sold per-washer**, not a one-time purchase. Scales by tier:
  - Vista 1500 $150 · Urban U1 $180 · Vista 2400 $750 · Urban U2 $900
  - Vista 3000 $1,500 · Urban Max $1,800 · Vista Pro $2,250 · Urban Stream $2,700
- **Soap nozzle is sold per-washer**, only on Medium tier and up:
  - Vista 2400 $375 · Urban U2 $450 · Vista 3000 $750 · Urban Max $900
  - Vista Pro $1,125 · Urban Stream $1,350
- Soap itself is free (no consumable cost)
- Turbo nozzle is sold per-washer ($15–$270 by tier)

### Extensions
- 3 extension types: Short, Long, Extra Long
- **Light-tier washers (Vista 1500, Urban U1) have only Long + Extra Long** — no Short
- **Extension cost ratio 1×:2×:4×** confirmed across all 8 washers
- Specific extension costs by washer (per moyens.net):
  - Vista 1500: Long $50 · Extra Long $100
  - Urban U1: Long $60 · Extra Long $120
  - Vista 2400: Short $125 · Long $250 · Extra Long $500
  - Urban U2: Short $150 · Long $300 · Extra Long $600
  - Vista 3000: Short $250 · Long $500 · Extra Long $1,000
  - Urban Max: Short $300 · Long $600 · Extra Long $1,200
  - Vista Pro: Short $375 · Long $750 · Extra Long $1,500
  - Urban Stream: Short $450 · Long $900 · Extra Long $1,800

### SwirlForce upgrades
- 17″ head $200 · 20″ head $400 · Short extension $100 · Long extension $300

### Currency & progression
- **Cash** is the only currency that gates gameplay progression
- **No star unlocks** in PWS2
- **No separate stat upgrades** (pressure/flow/tank) — buying a higher tier IS the upgrade
- **PowerWash Points** are cosmetic-only currency, **capped at 2,000**
- Money is earned only in Career Mode's first playthrough — replays of any kind pay only PowerWash Points
- Career Mode payout (Mount Rushless + prior missions) covers the full Maxed Out shop

### Co-op
- Up to 4 players in Story/Career Mode (split-screen + online)
- Up to 6 players in Free Play
- Cross-play, cross-saves
- Campaign progression is shared online for the first time
- Access equipment exists: ladders, scaffolds, scissor lifts, abseiling apparatus (level-provided)

### Brand differences (qualitative — well-supported by sources)
- Vista has wider spray pattern, lower raw pressure
- Urban has higher raw pressure, narrower spread
- Both reach the same endgame; Vista is $200–$2,000 cheaper per tier
- Splitting cash between brands "slows progression considerably"
